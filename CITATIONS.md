## Overview

This document catalogs the foundational scientific literature referenced during the founding session. The conversation spanned information physics, thermodynamics, neuroscience, phase-change materials, optical data storage, photonic computing, neuromorphic architectures, and LiFi communication — converging on the Uberbrain concept: a photonic neuromorphic computing architecture using multi-wavelength holographic storage in fused quartz with GST phase-change active memory.

References are organized by domain and numbered sequentially for cross-referencing with design documents, prototype specifications, and any future grant proposals.

## Priority Reading

For anyone wanting to understand the two strongest real-world pillars of this architecture:

- **Refs [13–15]** — Oxford/Exeter GST photonic neuromorphic work. All-optical memory and spiking networks in phase-change materials. This is the GST layer proven in lab.
- **Refs [16–17]** — Southampton 5D fused quartz storage. The quartz layer proven in lab. Ref [17] is the 360TB per disc result.

These four papers together establish that every major physical component of the Uberbrain has been demonstrated independently. The architecture is the integration.
